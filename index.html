NewsFlash is a tool to recommend free stock images for news articles, journals, or student essays. We analyze your text and recommend relevant images from Pixabay's collection of high quality photos and vector graphics based on the topics discussed in your articles. No need to go searching around Google Images or risk copyright infringement. We are your one stop shop for high quality public domain images to supplement your news and educational articles.

Tools used

This is the final project for Dr. James Caverlee's CSCE 470 class (Information Storage & Retrieval).
